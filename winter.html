Tour Highlights

This is not just a motorcycle trip; it's a high-altitude expedition through the remote and stunning Spiti Valley. Known as "The Middle Land," Spiti is a rugged, cold desert mountain valley that will test your limits and reward you with vistas that defy description.

  • Conquer the treacherous roads of Kinnaur Valley, including the famous cliffhanger section.
  • Ride to the last inhabited village near the Indo-Chinese border, Chitkul.
  • Send a postcard from the world's highest post office in Hikkim (14,400 ft).
  • Visit Komic, the highest village in the world connected by a motorable road (15,027 ft).
  • Explore the ancient Key Monastery, perched precariously on a hilltop.
  • Camp beside the mystical and serene Chandratal (Moon Lake).
  • Cross the high-altitude Kunzum Pass (4551m) and the iconic Atal Tunnel.

Our expert guides and mechanics ensure your safety and comfort as you navigate some of the world's most challenging and beautiful roads. This is a journey for the true explorer.

Detailed Itinerary

Welcome aboard, Rider! Your Himalayan adventure begins in Chandigarh. Arrive at the airport (IXC) or railway station, and our team will transfer you to the hotel. Check-in starts at 12 PM. Once settled, you're free to explore the city. Join us back at the hotel in the evening for a detailed orientation session and to meet your fellow riders. We'll cover the roadmap, riding etiquette, potential pitfalls, and allocate your motorbikes. Test them out and report any issues. Enjoy a delicious dinner with the group as you gear up for the journey ahead.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Dinner

After a hearty breakfast, we kickstart our epic ride! We leave the city plains behind, ascending into the Shivalik Hills. The ride is long but scenic as we cruise on smooth highways towards Shimla. We'll bypass the main city and continue our journey, descending towards the Sutlej river before climbing again into the lesser-known Seraj Valley. The last stretch of the ride takes us through dense pine forests and winding mountain roads, leading to the hidden gem of Jibhi. Upon arrival, relax, explore the nearby waterfall, and let the tranquility of the mountain paradise sink in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What kind of riding skill is needed?

A: You should be a confident rider with at least 2-3 years of experience. This route includes challenging off-road sections, water crossings, and narrow mountain roads.

Q: What about Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS)?

A: We have designed the itinerary for gradual acclimatization. However, AMS is a risk. We advise consulting your doctor for preventative medication like Diamox.
