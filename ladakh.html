Tour Highlights

This is the bucket-list Himalayan adventure. Our 10-day Classic Ladakh tour is designed for those who want to experience all the iconic locations of this magical land. Flying into Leh gives us more time to explore the heart of Ladakh, with a focus on proper acclimatization and maximum adventure.

  • Fly into Leh (3,500m) with the first two days dedicated to acclimatization.
  • Ride to the top of **Khardung La (5,359m)**, one of the world's highest motorable passes.
  • Explore the high-altitude desert of **Nubra Valley** and ride a Bactrian (double-humped) camel.
  • Visit the 14th-century Diskit Monastery and its giant Maitreya Buddha statue.
  • Camp on the shores of the breathtaking, color-changing **Pangong Tso Lake**.
  • Cross the mighty **Chang La Pass (5,360m)**.
  • Visit the famous monasteries of the Indus Valley: Thiksey, Hemis, and Shey.
  • Experience the unique culture and warm hospitality of the Ladakhi people.

This tour combines moderate riding with incredible cultural experiences, making it the perfect first-time expedition to the high Himalayas.

Detailed Itinerary

Julley! Welcome to Leh. You will be picked up from Leh Airport (IXL) and transferred to our hotel. The day is for complete rest. This is critical for acclimatization to the high altitude (3,500m). In the evening, we'll have our group briefing, introductions, and bike allocation.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Dinner

After breakfast, we'll take a short "warm-up" ride around Leh to get comfortable with the bikes. We'll visit Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, and the Hall of Fame. This short ride helps with acclimatization and allows you to flag any issues with your motorcycle. Evening free to explore Leh market.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

Our first proper ride. We head out on the Leh-Srinagar highway. We'll visit the Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, experience the gravity-defying Magnetic Hill, and witness the stunning confluence (Sangam) of the Indus and Zanskar rivers. We'll also visit the Alchi Monastery before riding back to Leh.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

The day you've been waiting for! We ride to the top of Khardung La (5,359m), the gateway to the Shyok and Nubra valleys. After celebrating at the top, we descend into the Nubra Valley, a stunning high-altitude desert. We'll reach our camp/hotel in Hunder and visit the famous sand dunes for a camel ride.

Accommodation: Deluxe Camp / Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

A relaxed day exploring Nubra. We'll visit the 14th-century Diskit Monastery and its 106-foot tall Maitreya Buddha statue, which offers panoramic views of the valley. You can also opt for an optional ride to the border village of Turtuk (requires an early start).

Accommodation: Deluxe Camp / Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

We ride along the remote and beautiful Shyok River road, a stunning route connecting Nubra directly to Pangong. The landscape is dramatic and ever-changing. We'll arrive at the magnificent Pangong Tso Lake (4,250m) by late afternoon, just in time to see its colors change with the setting sun.

Accommodation: Deluxe Camp Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

Waking up to the serene beauty of Pangong Lake is unforgettable. After breakfast, we start our ride back to Leh. This time, we cross the mighty Chang La Pass (5,360m). We'll be back in Leh by the afternoon, with time to relax and rest.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

Today we explore the cultural heart of Ladakh. We ride to two of the most famous monasteries: Thiksey (a mini-Potala Palace) and Hemis (the largest and wealthiest monastery in Ladakh). We'll also visit the Shey Palace. It's a day of culture and beautiful riding along the Indus River.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

We keep this day as a buffer for any weather delays or as a free day for you to explore, shop for souvenirs, or simply relax. You could also opt for a thrilling white-water rafting trip on the Zanskar River (at extra cost). In the evening, we host our grand farewell dinner to celebrate our incredible journey.

Accommodation: Deluxe Hotel Meals: Breakfast and Dinner

After breakfast, our team will transfer you to Leh Airport (IXL) for your flight home. You leave with incredible memories, photos, and the spirit of the high Himalayas. Julley!

Accommodation: NIL Meals: Breakfast

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best way to handle acclimatization?

A: Flying into Leh requires at least 24-36 hours of complete rest. Our itinerary includes two nights in Leh before any major pass-crossing, which is the best way to acclimatize. Drink plenty of water and avoid over-exertion.

Q: Is this ride suitable for a pillion?

A: Yes, this tour is very suitable for a pillion rider. The roads are challenging but generally well-paved on the main routes. The "Moderate" rating means it's a good balance of adventure and comfort.
