Explore Your Ride

From solo thrills to group comfort, travel in your own style.

Adventure Bikes

Experience the raw beauty of the Himalayas on the iconic Royal Enfield Himalayan. These tours are designed for true adventurers seeking the thrill of navigating challenging terrains and high mountain passes on two wheels. Our bikes are meticulously maintained and equipped for the journey.

  • Robust Royal Enfield Himalayan bikes for all terrains.
  • Experienced lead riders and support staff.
  • Mechanic with spare parts on all tours.
  • Ideal for solo riders or small groups of experienced bikers.

4x4 Cars

For those who prefer comfort without compromising on adventure, our 4x4 car tours offer the perfect solution. Travel in rugged yet comfortable vehicles, driven by experienced local drivers who know the mountain roads inside out. Ideal for families, couples, or anyone seeking a relaxed pace.

  • Comfortable and reliable 4x4 vehicles (e.g., Mahindra Thar, Scorpio).
  • Professional, safe, and knowledgeable local drivers.
  • Enjoy panoramic views from the comfort of your seat.
  • Access remote locations without the physical demands of biking.

Campervans

Take your home with you on the road with our fully equipped campervans. Perfect for ultimate freedom and flexibility, these vehicles allow you to choose your own campsite under the stars, wake up to breathtaking views, and explore off the beaten path at your leisure.

  • Integrated kitchen, sleeping areas, and basic amenities.
  • Freedom to design your own itinerary and overnight stops.
  • Perfect for couples or small families seeking independence.
  • A unique and immersive way to experience nature.

Mini Buses

For larger groups, clubs, or corporate teams, our mini-bus options provide comfortable and efficient transport for your entire expedition. With ample space for passengers and luggage, you can journey together, share the experience, and leave the driving to our professional team.

  • Spacious and comfortable mini-buses for larger groups.
  • Professional and experienced drivers for safe travel.
  • Cost-effective solution for group travel.
  • Ideal for custom tours, events, or private group adventures.
