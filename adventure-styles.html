An Adventure for Everyone

From solo journeys to group adventures, every trail is open to you.

Solo Travelers

Joining one of our guided tours as a solo traveler is the best way to experience the Himalayas. You get the freedom of the open road without the stress of logistics, and you instantly become part of a community of like-minded adventurers.

  • Safety in numbers with expert guides and a backup vehicle.
  • Meet new people from around the world and forge lifelong friendships.
  • We handle all permits, accommodations, and bike maintenance.
  • Option for a single-occupancy room for your own private space.

Family Adventures

Create unforgettable memories with a Himalayan adventure designed for your family's safety and comfort. We offer private tours in comfortable 4x4 vehicles, allowing you to witness the stunning landscapes at your own pace, with itineraries that are exciting for all ages.

  • Safe, comfortable, and reliable 4x4 vehicles with expert local drivers.
  • Flexible itineraries with time for stops, photos, and exploration.
  • Hand-picked, family-friendly hotels and guesthouses.
  • We manage all logistics so you can focus on the experience.

Small Groups & Friends

Have your own riding club, a group of friends, or colleagues? We specialize in creating custom-tailored private tours. Whether you want to conquer the toughest passes or take a scenic, leisurely route, we can build the perfect expedition just for your group.

  • Completely customized itinerary based on your group's skill and interest.
  • Private tour leader, mechanic, and support vehicle.
  • Flexible dates that work for your schedule.
  • Exclusive group pricing and accommodation options.

Corporate Retreats

Break the boardroom monotony with an expedition that will truly build your team. Our corporate retreats are designed to foster leadership, resilience, and camaraderie in one of the world's most inspiring environments. We handle everything from luxury stays to adventure activities.

  • All-inclusive packages including premium stays, food, and transport.
  • Guided adventure activities (motorcycling, 4x4, trekking).
  • Team-building workshops and conference facilities available.
  • A unique and unforgettable way to reward and inspire your team.
