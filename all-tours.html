Detailed Itinerary

Overnight at Volvo bus.

Arrive in Manali, meet the team, get your bike, and start the ride towards Jispa via the Atal Tunnel.

Cross the high Shingo La pass and enter the remote Zanskar Valley, reaching Padum by evening.

A long day of riding across the Zanskar landscape, crossing Pensi La and passing the Drang-Drung Glacier.

Ride on the Leh-Srinagar highway, visit the Lamayuru "Moonland" monastery, and reach Leh.

Ride to the top of Khardung La and descend into the high-altitude desert of Nubra Valley.

Take the remote Shyok river road to connect directly to the stunning Pangong Tso Lake.

An extreme ride through remote eastern Ladakh. Visit Rizangla war memorial and conquer the mighty Umling La, one of the world's highest motorable roads.

Ride back to Leh from the remote Koyul region, acclimatizing to the journey.

Begin the journey back on the Manali-Leh highway, crossing high passes and reaching Jispa for the night.

Ride from Jispa to Manali via the Atal Tunnel. In the evening, board the overnight Volvo bus to Delhi.

Arrive in Delhi in the morning. The tour concludes.
